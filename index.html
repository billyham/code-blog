Something about me

A careful disassembly and reassembly of a Panasonic HVX200 video camera in order to remove the compensator lens element from the camera's fixed lens to allow for very close up focusing. With a meticulous attention to detail, I explored the inner workings of a professional HD camera. Although my goal to entirely remove the lens wasn't achievable, I succeeded at modifying the internal lens enough to enable the camera to be used for recording the individual images on the surface of a film.
