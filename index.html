A little about me

I am an iOS and web developer with experience in managing teams and applying a keen eye for design details and engaging user experiences. I use common design patterns to write reliable code and as a tenacious problem solver I am eager to work on complex applications. I am versed in JavaScript, Objective-C and Swift, I recently updated my skills at Code Fellows, a web development bootcamp.

At my job and in my personal hobbies I pursue creative endeavors at the intersection of art and technology. I have a passion for physical computing – creating hybrid projects that incorporate software development – and hardware hacking – deconstructing things like cameras and lenses to explore their inner workings and altering their function. I am a lens hacker and I teach workshops on making homemade lenses for DSLR cameras.
